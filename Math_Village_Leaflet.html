Inside · Features

What's in
the build.

i. Twenty Mini-Games

Installable browser games covering counting, addition, fractions, decimals and algebra basics — all running fully offline against IndexedDB.

ii. Adaptive Learning Engine

A 13-skill knowledge DAG plus Bayesian Knowledge Tracing estimates per-skill mastery and targets each student's Zone of Proximal Development.

iii. Teacher Dashboard

Mastery heatmap, weakness alerts and a fair Bayesian-shrinkage leaderboard — ranking by depth and breadth of mastery, never raw XP alone.

iv. Spaced Repetition

The SM-2 algorithm schedules reviews of mastered skills above 0.85 mastery, defending learning against forgetting over time.

Under the Hood

Tech
stack.

Frontend

React 19 + Vite 8 + Tailwind CSS, with Zustand for state, idb for IndexedDB and vite-plugin-pwa for the service worker, manifest and offline cache.

Backend

Express 4 + Mongoose 8 on MongoDB Atlas, with JWT auth, cross-device sync, and a teacher class-analytics endpoint powering the dashboard.

Adaptive ML

BKT ships as the live default; an optional DKT backend (TensorFlow.js LSTM) is a drop-in via VITE_MASTERY_BACKEND=dkt.

Evaluation

A Python harness (numpy · pandas · scikit-learn) generates synthetic trajectories and reports next-correct AUC, A/B learning gains and component ablations.

The Learning Loop

Assess.
Adapt.
Master.

Stage 01 · Assess

Estimate mastery.

BKT updates per-skill mastery after every answer; the knowledge graph surfaces prerequisites so weaknesses are caught at their source.

Stage 02 · Adapt

Target the ZPD.

Difficulty serves Easy below 0.40 mastery, Medium between 0.40 and 0.75, and Hard above 0.75 — challenging but always achievable.

Stage 03 · Master

Defend the gains.

SM-2 schedules spaced reviews of mastered skills and a fair-ranking leaderboard rewards breadth and consistency over lucky bursts of XP.
